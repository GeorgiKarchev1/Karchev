# Karchx Reporting Stack

Prepared on May 12, 2026.

This file turns the SEO strategy into a repeatable operating system.

## 1. Core Principle

Do not judge SEO by vibes.

Track:

- pages
- queries
- positions
- clicks
- leads

The minimum useful loop is weekly.

## 2. Required Free Stack

These should be in place before paying for bigger SEO software:

- Google Search Console
- Google Analytics 4
- Google Trends
- Google Keyword Planner

Why:

- GSC gives real impressions, clicks, CTR, and average position from Google.
- GA4 gives landing-page behavior and conversion tracking.
- Trends helps compare demand by country and topic direction.
- Keyword Planner helps estimate demand and discover related commercial queries.

## 3. Suggested Paid Stack for This Site

For `karchx.com`, the best starting rule is:

- do not buy an expensive all-in-one suite immediately
- buy paid SEO software only after GSC has at least a few weeks of useful data

Recommended order:

1. Free stack first
2. One rank tracking + keyword research platform
3. Only later, backlink or larger competitive tooling if needed

## 4. Best-Fit Tool Path

### Lean path

Use:

- GSC
- GA4
- Keyword Planner
- Google Trends

Good if:

- budget is tight
- the site is still early
- the first 90 days are mostly about service pages, indexing, and early traction

### Practical paid path

Use:

- free Google stack
- one platform for rank tracking + keyword research

Recommended first paid candidate:

- SE Ranking

Reason:

- stronger value than enterprise-priced suites for a smaller site
- daily keyword tracking
- keyword research
- competitor research
- audit
- reporting integrations

## 5. Weekly Reporting Template

Every Monday, update:

### Market snapshots

- BG impressions
- BG clicks
- BG average position
- EN impressions
- EN clicks
- EN average position

### Page tracking

For each core page:

- target URL
- impressions
- clicks
- CTR
- average position
- leads if applicable

### Keyword buckets

- BG service keywords
- BG article keywords
- EN service keywords
- EN article keywords
- branded queries

### Wins / losses

- pages with biggest impression growth
- pages with weak CTR
- keywords entering positions `6` to `20`
- pages receiving impressions but no clicks

## 6. Monthly SEO Review

At the end of each month:

- identify the top `3` pages by impression growth
- identify the top `3` pages by lead contribution
- identify the top `5` queries that need better titles or descriptions
- identify the next `2` articles to publish based on GSC query demand
- identify the next `1` service page or case study to improve

## 7. What Not to Pay For Yet

Avoid paying early for:

- enterprise SEO suites
- expensive backlink platforms if no outreach system exists yet
- AI SEO dashboards that look impressive but do not change execution
- multiple overlapping tools that duplicate keyword and rank data

## 8. Immediate Next Setup Tasks

1. Verify domain property in Google Search Console.
2. Link GSC to GA4.
3. Track lead conversions in GA4 for:
   - contact submissions
   - estimate completions
   - booked calls if possible
4. Load the keyword set from `data/seo-keywords.json` into your tracking process.
